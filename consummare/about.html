Consummare Inc.

We offer OEM design and production of ultra-high quality boxes for the presentation of luxury goods and consumables. Our precise and very beautiful boxes are eminently suitable for watches, jewelry, gemstones, pearls, coins, trophies and plaques and equally so for wines, liqueurs, chocolates, caviar and more. Sometimes our boxes might cost more than the item being presented, contributing significantly to the actual and perceived value of the gift or product.
Bring your own designs or collaborate with us to make drop- dead-gorgeous packaging to present your work at its ultimate best. We have a wealth of standard designs to choose from or modify or we can develop something that is exclusively and perfectly suited to your requirements.
